# How to Create a YOURDLE
• Enter an answer in the "Enter..." field, then press the Enter/Return Key.
• Save the answer by clicking the checkmark button.
• To unsave an answer, click the refresh button. The refresh button also puts entered answers inside the tiles.
• To add more answers, click the plus sign button.
• To have less answers, click the minus sign button.
• Submit the answers by clicking on "Finish".
• Answers can be 3 to 7 letters long. 
NOTE: Answers will be deleted by the end of the day. Also, answers cannot be edited once submitted.
